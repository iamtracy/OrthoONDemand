About Ortho ON DEMAND

As a laboratory technologist, you're being tasked to do much more with a lot less. That means fewer resources for just about everything-including opportunities for meaningful engagement with colleagues and experts in the field. Ortho ON DEMAND can help.

We provide 24/7 access to live webcasts, on-demand videos, peer-to-peer chats, articles and case studies. Learn from key opinion leaders and engage with colleagues. Ortho ON DEMAND offers content for professionals in both the transfusion medicine and clinical laboratories industries. Your commitment to your patients is our inspiration.
